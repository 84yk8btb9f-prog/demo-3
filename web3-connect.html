Frequently Asked Questions

What is a crypto wallet?

A crypto wallet stores your private keys and allows you to send, receive, and manage your cryptocurrencies and NFTs securely.

How do I connect my wallet?

Click on your preferred wallet above. A popup will appear asking you to authorize the connection. Follow the prompts to complete.

Is it safe to connect my wallet?

Yes. We never store your private keys or seed phrases. The connection is encrypted and follows industry security standards.

What are the fees?

CryptoVerse charges 2.5% on sales. You'll also pay gas fees to the Ethereum network when minting or transferring NFTs.
